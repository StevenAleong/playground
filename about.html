About

Born in 1984 in the outskirts of Canada in a small town called Ottawa. I moved to a bigger city when I was 10 years old, to Edmonton with dreams of becoming a professional bodyguard but couldn't get hired because of an injury I sustained to my subclavius muscle.

With that dream all but crushed, my friend guided me to pursue a lucrative career in programming. After recovering, I spent every moment learning and developing all that I could imagine. Since then, I haven't looked back and continue to move forward towards becoming the ultimate programmer or at least one that doesn't ever stop learning and creating new and exciting things.
